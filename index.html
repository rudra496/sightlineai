The Challenge

Visually impaired individuals in developing countries face severe daily challenges

Exclusion from Society

Limited participation in education, employment, and social activities due to mobility barriers

Dependence on Others

Rely on family members or caregivers for daily navigation and tasks

Risk of Injury

Higher accident rates due to inability to detect obstacles in real-time

Limited Solutions

Existing assistive technologies cost $3,000-$4,500, making them inaccessible

Our Solution

Affordable, AI-powered wearable device that enables independent navigation

Key Features

  • Real-time object detection and obstacle avoidance
  • Text and currency recognition for reading signs and banknotes
  • Local language voice commands (Bengali and English)
  • Offline capability works without internet connection
  • Emergency SOS alert to preset contacts
  • Lightweight, comfortable wearable design
